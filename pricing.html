Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use DroneDeploy for free?

Yes! The DroneDeploy Explorer plan enables you to create 2D maps and 3D models without charge. You get some of the great functionality offered by the platform, such as automated flying, image processing, and sharing and annotating your maps; but are limited in terms of the number of maps you can process and other advanced capabilities (volumetric calculations, data exports, etc.). See the Explorer Plan for details.

What are the payment options for DroneDeploy?

DroneDeploy accepts most Credit and Debit Cards. All subscriptions will be charged in U.S. Dollars. We currently accept:

MasterCard, Visa, American Express, Discover and Diners Club

Can I trial the "Business" or "Premier" DroneDeploy subscriptions instead of the "Pro" plan?

Please contact sales@dronedeploy.com to inquire about testing the capabilities offered within the Business and Premier plans.

Is there a discount for nonprofits, educational institutions and students?

Yes! If your status can be verified you are eligible for a discount. Please contact sales@dronedeploy.com to learn more.

Can I purchase one month of DroneDeploy?

When you subscribe to DroneDeploy, you can select annual or monthly payments. If you select monthly payments, you will be enrolled for automatic payments at the start of each month. You can discontinue your subscription at any point in time by cancelling your subscription before the next recurring payment. However, by downgrading or cancelling your subscription you will lose functionality (such as data exporting, analysis capabilities or resolution) in accordance with your new subscription plan, or free Explorer plan.

How do I change or cancel my DroneDeploy subscription?

You can change or cancel your subscription at any time from the Account Settings within DroneDeploy. Any upgrade changes you make will be reflected immediately and the current month will be prorated accordingly. Any downgrade changes, including cancellations, will be reflected at the end of your existing billing cycle.

Can I still access my data if I cancel my paid subscription?

Upon cancelling your subscription, your account will be moved to our free Explorer plan. As a result, you will be able to access and view all of your previously created 2D maps and 3D models, as well as any annotations you added. However, all your data will be limited to the features offered within the free Explorer plan, which does not include NDVI, elevation data, data exports and more. See the Explorer Plan for details.
